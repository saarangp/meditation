History of Meditation

Meditation has been around for millenia, with the earliest practices in India as early as 5000 BCE, according to Psychology Today. It spread across the globe to many different cultures and is often found as a key component to many religious and spritual practices. At some point, we forgot about self-reflecting and became self-absorbing. Things are changing, however. Fueled by growing awareness for mental health, mindfulness activities are taking the world, and the App Store, by storm.

Headspace is an awesome mobile app and is an excellent point to get started on learning more about meditation and mindfulness. There are tons of tutorials and guided practices to help you develop good meditation and mindfulness practices that are easy to do in the squalm of the day to day.
